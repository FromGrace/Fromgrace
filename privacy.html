2. Server Log Files

When accessing this website, technical data is automatically processed (e.g. IP address, date and time of access, browser type, operating system, accessed pages). Processing is carried out pursuant to Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR based on our legitimate interest in ensuring the secure and stable operation of the website. Log data is deleted regularly and is not combined with other data sources.

3. Embedded Content (YouTube / Bandcamp)

This website includes content from external platforms, in particular YouTube (Google Ireland Limited) and Bandcamp.

For data protection reasons, YouTube videos are not loaded automatically. A connection to YouTube servers is only established after you have given your explicit consent to the use of marketing cookies via our cookie consent tool.

After consent has been given, personal data (e.g. IP address, device information, accessed page) may be transmitted to Google. This may also involve a transfer of data to the United States, where an adequate level of data protection comparable to the EU cannot be guaranteed. The legal basis for processing is Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. Consent can be withdrawn at any time via the cookie settings.

Bandcamp content is only loaded when you actively open a Bandcamp link. In this case, data may be processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Further information:
YouTube / Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Bandcamp: https://bandcamp.com/privacy

4. Google Fonts

This website uses external fonts provided by Google Fonts (provider: Google Ireland Limited). When loading these fonts, a connection to Google servers is established, which may involve the transmission of your IP address. The use of Google Fonts is based on your consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

5. Cookies & Consent Management

We use the cookie consent tool “CookieYes”, provided by Mozilor Limited (Ireland), to manage user consent. CookieYes stores your preferences in order to operate this website in a legally compliant manner. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

You may change or withdraw your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie banner.

6. Contact by Email

If you contact us by email, the personal data you provide will be processed for the purpose of handling your request pursuant to Art. 6(1)(b) or (f) GDPR and deleted once it is no longer required.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, rectification or erasure of your personal data, restriction of processing, data portability, and to object to the processing of personal data. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.
