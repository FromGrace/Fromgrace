2. Server Log Files

When accessing this website, technical data (such as IP address, date and time of access, browser type and accessed pages) is automatically processed by our hosting provider on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and stable website operation). The log data is deleted at regular intervals and is not merged with other data sources.

3. Embedded Content (YouTube / Bandcamp)

This website may display embedded content from external platforms, in particular YouTube (Google Ireland Limited) and Bandcamp (Epic Games Commerce GmbH / USA).

For data protection reasons, YouTube videos on this website are not loaded automatically. They are only activated after your explicit consent via our cookie consent tool (CookieYes). Before consent is given, no connection to YouTube is established and no cookies are set.

Once consent has been given, a connection to YouTube may be established and personal data may be processed by Google (e.g. IP address, device data, page accessed). If you are logged into a Google account, your activity may be associated with your profile. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. Consent may be withdrawn at any time via the cookie banner.

Bandcamp content is only loaded when the respective link is opened. In this case, data may be processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in promoting our music releases).

Further information:
YouTube / Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Bandcamp: https://bandcamp.com/privacy

4. Contact by Email

If you contact us via email, the personal data you provide will be processed for the purpose of handling your inquiry pursuant to Art. 6(1)(b) or (f) GDPR and deleted when it is no longer required.

5. Cookies & Consent Management

This website uses a cookie consent tool (CookieYes), allowing you to choose whether external services (such as YouTube) may be activated. Your preferences can be changed or withdrawn at any time via the cookie banner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, rectification or erasure of your personal data, restriction of processing, data portability and to object to processing in accordance with the provisions of the GDPR. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
